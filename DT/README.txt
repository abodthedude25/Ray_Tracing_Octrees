DTVerts.csv:

MESH_NUM: index of mesh that vertex belongs to.
VERTEX NUMBER: vertex index in mesh, this is what is referenced in DTFaces.csv.
EASTING: easting coordinate of vertex.
NORTHING: northing coordinate of vertex.
ELEVATION: elevation of vertex.
LATITUDE: latitude of vertex.
LONGITUDE: longitude of vertex.
ELEV_MIN: minimum elevation of mesh.

DTFaces.csv:

MESH_NUM: index of mesh that face belongs to.

V1, V2, V3: 3 vertex indices cooresponding to traingular face.
